# **App Name**: Julio Bodero Portfolio

## Core Features:

- Professional Hero Section: Display full name, title, and a brief, impactful tagline to immediately capture the attention of recruiters and employers.
- Skills Showcase: A grid or card layout clearly displaying technical skills, categorized by Frontend, Backend, DevOps, Cloud, Architecture, Messaging, Databases, ERP/CRM, and Version Control.
- Dynamic CV Generation: Generates and updates the CV (resume) in real time based on changes the user makes using the GUI. Enables exporting to PDF.
- Key Achievements: Quantifiable achievements section to highlight specific successes (e.g., "30% efficiency increase with CI/CD").
- Experience Timeline: Interactive timeline displaying professional experience, with details on roles, responsibilities, and technologies used at each company.
- Contact Form & Information: A contact section with a form for direct inquiries, plus visible email, phone number, LinkedIn profile link, and location.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A professional, calming blue (#29ABE2) to instill trust and reliability.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5), very lightly tinted with blue. A soft, neutral tone to ensure readability and a clean aesthetic.
- Accent color: A deep sky blue (#034A8F). A darker shade of blue is chosen for highlights and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', sans-serif, known for its readability and modern appearance, ensuring a professional and clean look.
- Subtle hover effects and scroll animations to add a touch of interactivity without overwhelming the user.
- Clean, sectioned layout, with generous use of whitespace to ensure easy readability and a professional aesthetic.
- Professional, minimalist icons to represent different skills and sections within the portfolio. Consider feathericons.
